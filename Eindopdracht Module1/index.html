Urban jungle

Plants aren't just plants, at least not for everybody. Some people make a living out of it, others enjoy it as decoration. Where ever you go you will find an effort of people to connect with nature in their homes or workplace.

Think about it: Isn't your room way nicer when you have some flowers or plants to accompany you?

"To plant a garden is to believe in tomorrow" - Audrey Hepburn

I launched this page to show people you have many options to make your home feel more cosy and especially for the urban people to add some nature!

Feel free to take a look at my home-owned plants and ofcourse contact me!
